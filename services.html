Spiritual Direction & Soul Care

Grounded, confidential spaces for honest conversation and discernment.

Core Offerings

Spiritual Direction

A monthly or bi-monthly session focused on noticing God’s presence, exploring your inner landscape, and discerning next steps.

Format: 50–60 minutes · In-person or virtual

Pastoral Counseling

Support for seasons of grief, transition, burnout, relational strain, or spiritual disorientation.

Format: 50–60 minutes

Leadership Soul Care

A reflective space for leaders, founders, pastors, and caregivers who carry responsibility for others and need a place to be fully human.

Focus: identity, calling, burnout, complexity, clarity

Fees & Accessibility

I work with a range of financial situations and don’t want cost to be the barrier that keeps you from the care you need.

  • Standard Rate — for those with stable income and resources
  • Sliding Scale — for those in transition or with limited margin
  • Scholarship / Pay-What-You-Can — for those in significant financial strain

No one is turned away for inability to pay.

How Sessions Work

  • Sessions are conversational, reflective, and guided by what you bring.
  • Silence, prayer, and contemplative practices may be used as appropriate.
  • You set the pace; I accompany you.
  • We regularly check in on how the work is landing for you.
