Why BoldHearts Exists

A grounded space for those carrying more than they can name.

The Heart Behind BoldHearts

BoldHearts was created for people who carry weight—emotional, spiritual, relational, vocational—and need a place where they don’t have to hold it alone.

This work is rooted in the belief that God is already gently at work in every life. Spiritual direction and pastoral care simply help you notice that movement, name what is true, and discern the next step with courage.

About Steve

I’m Steve Forney—a spiritual director, pastoral counselor, and long-time guide for leaders navigating complexity. My background spans organizational leadership, systems design, and soul care.

I’ve walked with people through seasons of transition, burnout, calling shifts, grief, and deep spiritual questions. My approach is contemplative, trauma-aware, and rooted in the Christian tradition, while welcoming people from a wide range of backgrounds and beliefs.
