About BoldHearts

BoldHearts exists to offer a quiet, grounded space for those seeking clarity, healing, and spiritual direction. My work is rooted in presence, deep listening, and the belief that every person carries a story worth honoring.

I walk with individuals who are navigating transition, loss, calling, burnout, or simply the desire to hear God’s voice more clearly. My approach is gentle, attentive, and shaped by years of pastoral experience
